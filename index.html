About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of the Bundeswehr Munich and TUM, with a primary research focus on cross-view geo-localisation. Currently, I am delving into how this domain can benefit from advancements in other areas. Specifically, I am exploring the synergies between cross-view geo-localisation and multimodal deep learning, as well as the potential enhancements image synthesis can bring to the field.

If you want to collaborate just write me a mail.

Research Interests

  • Computer Vision: image retrieval, cross-view image matching, image synthesis
  • Multimodal Deep Learning: multimodal representations, large multimodal models
  • Machine Learning: contrastive learning, representation learning
